What is PPQ?

PPQ (Provisioning Profile Query) is an Apple service. For developer teams created after June 6, 2021, apps signed with development or ad-hoc provisioning profiles must check in with ppq.apple.com when first launched. This verifies the signing certificate and provisioning profile.

If the device is offline or the check fails, the app may not open. PPQ helps prevent issues if the certificate or profile has been flagged, revoked, or otherwise restricted by Apple. Offline provisioning profiles can work for up to 7 days before re-checking is required.
